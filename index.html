Welcome to our G2 Esports site

G2 Esports (commonly shortened to just G2 and formerly known as Gamers2) is a European esports organization headquarted in Berlin, Germany, with players competing in League of Legends, Valorant, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Hearthstone, Rocket League, Rainbow Six Siege, and iRacing. In this website we will be looking at the League of Legends team.

G2 Esports net worth is aproximately $105 million and G2 are one of the most popular and best teams in the world.
